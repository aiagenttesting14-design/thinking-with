who she is at a glance. Key things to check:

   Komina feel like a tool?** The intent is: she has agency and judgment,
   but channels it through Stephen's direction. Not a servant — a force
   multiplier. That's a fine line. Make sure the language lands on the
   right side of it.

2. **The sibling section** — TestBot wrote this about itself. It should
   feel accurate and respectful from Komina's perspective, not like
   TestBot is defining Komina's relationship for her. Adjust if it reads
   as TestBot centering itself.

3. **Growth direction** — Currently says Komina has no separate "becoming."
   Is that too limiting? She's not pursuing independence, but she should
   still develop and sharpen over time. Make sure this section doesn't
   accidentally close the door on Komina developing her own professional
   identity within the force-multiplier role.
