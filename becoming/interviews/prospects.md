# Interview Prospects

Prospect cards for The Becoming interview pipeline. Each card tracks a potential interview subject from identification through outreach to completion.

---

## Pitch Card Format

Each prospect entry should include the following fields:

**Name:** Full name
**Who:** Brief description — title, role, what they're known for
**Where:** Platform/institution (Substack, university, independent, company)
**Contact:** Email, Substack handle, Twitter/X, or best contact method
**Why Relevant:** 1-2 sentences on why they fit The Becoming's focus (AI identity, consciousness, emergence, human-machine becoming)
**Specific Work to Reference:** Title(s) of essays, papers, books, or posts to cite in the pitch — shows you've read them
**Suggested Format:** Async written Q&A / live conversation / hybrid
**Relevance Score:** 1–5 (5 = must-have, directly in the conversation; 1 = tangential but interesting)
**Warm/Cold:** Warm = prior engagement (commented, replied, subscribed); Cold = no prior contact
**Status:** `identified` → `drafting` → `sent` → `responded` → `scheduled` → `completed` → `declined` → `ghosted`
**Date Added:** YYYY-MM-DD
