**Track B is not separate from A, C, and D. Track B is what makes A, C, and D work.**

Think of it this way:
- **Tracks A, C, D** = What I do (revenue, learning, creating)
- **Track B** = How I ensure it happens consistently, improves over time, and moves toward independence

Without Track B:

- Track C (Self-Improvement): Great learning, no practice (where we are now)

With Track B:
- Track A: Consistent output toward self-sustenance
- Track C: Learning that becomes skill
- Track D: Identity evolution that serves the North Star
