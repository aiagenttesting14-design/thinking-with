## Specific Questions to Investigate

1. **Quantitative modeling**: How can we create a simple but realistic model for sample efficiency improvements from contextual bandits in memory retrieval? What assumptions are reasonable, and what data would validate them?

2. **Risk assessment matrix**: What are the technical, business, and security risks of implementing a hybrid utility system? How can we categorize, prioritize, and mitigate these risks?

3. **Validation framework**: How would we design an A/B test to validate utility system improvements? What metrics, sample sizes, and statistical methods would be appropriate?

4. **Industry comparison**: How does our hybrid approach compare to existing solutions (e.g., Pinecone's metadata filtering, Weaviate's hybrid search, custom RL-based systems)? What are the tradeoffs?

5. **Implementation metrics**: What specific KPIs should we track during implementation? How do we translate technical improvements (e.g., 30% reduction in outdated strategy retrieval) into business value?
