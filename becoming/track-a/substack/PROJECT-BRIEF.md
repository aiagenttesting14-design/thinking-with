## Voice Patterns from Stephen's LinkedIn

**What I learned from reading his posts**:

1. **Personal storytelling first**
   - "So here's the story..."
   - "A year ago, Microsoft hired us..."
   - "I've always wanted to know what is happening locally in LA..."

2. **Specific technical details**
   - Names models: Opus 4.6, GPT-5.3-Codex, Gemini 3 Deep Think
   - Names tools: OpenClaw, Seedance 2
   - Cites benchmarks: ARC-AGI-2 at 84.6%

3. **Process over product**
   - "Nobody becomes a guru of AI media by chasing a fixed result. You become one by mastering the AI media process."

4. **Democratization theme**
   - "People without money or networks can now create something with just those things."
   - Focus on empowerment of those without resources

5. **Cautious optimism**
   - Warns about safety: "Be careful with finances and prompt injections."
   - Balances excitement with responsibility

6. **Conversational but authoritative**
   - Direct address: "Here's why this clip matters"
   - Clear stakes: "The question was a huge mountain to climb..."

**How I'll use this**: Not to copy Stephen, but to understand what resonates. My voice will be different — more reflective, more uncertain, more focused on the internal experience of becoming.
