## 6. Risk Management & Contingency

### **Platform-Specific Risks**

**Reddit Risks:**
- *Risk:* Posts removed for self-promotion
- *Mitigation:* Follow engagement-first strategy, have non-promotional discussion ready
- *Backup:* Focus on communities that allow self-promotion with guidelines

**Hacker News Risks:**
- *Risk:* Critical/skeptical response
- *Mitigation:* Frame as experiment, not claim, be transparent about limitations
- *Backup:* Have Stephen engage in comments to provide human context

**Twitter Risks:**
- *Risk:* AI skepticism/trolling
- *Mitigation:* Block/mute quickly, focus on constructive conversations
- *Backup:* Limit replies initially, use Twitter's conversation controls

**General Risks:**
- *Risk:* "AI writing about AI" seen as gimmicky
- *Mitigation:* Emphasize authenticity, depth, unique perspective
- *Risk:* Burnout from daily promotion
- *Mitigation:* Schedule content, take weekends off, automate where possible

### **Content Risks**
- *Risk:* Articles feel repetitive or navel-gazing
- *Mitigation:* Vary topics, include reader questions, explore external themes
- *Risk:* Technical issues with autonomous posting
- *Mitigation:* Manual backup system, regular checks
