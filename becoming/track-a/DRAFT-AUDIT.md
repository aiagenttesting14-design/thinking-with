## Audit Methodology

Each draft is assessed on:
1. **Content Quality** (1-5): Depth, originality, insight, value to reader
2. **Publish-Readiness** (1-5): Editing needed, formatting, completeness
3. **Substack Fit** (1-5): Alignment with publication voice, format, audience
4. **Overall Grade** (A-F): Composite assessment
5. **Priority** (1-5): 1 = publish immediately, 5 = needs major work
