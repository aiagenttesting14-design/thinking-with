Dignity • Embodiment • Love • Transcendence • Agency

Notre Dame's faith-based approach to AI ethics goes beyond "safety and privacy" to ask deeper questions:
- Does this honor human dignity?
- Does this respect embodied, relational reality?
- Does this foster genuine care?

- Does this preserve human moral responsibility?

**Why it matters**: This is what "doing things the right way" looks like at scale. And it starts with exactly your values.

## Ten Minutes: Do This

### Try the Presence Check
Before your next AI interaction:

1. **Three breaths** — slow down
2. **Notice**: Am I rushed or present?
3. **Ask**: What do I actually want to discover here?
4. **Commit**: I will notice what surprises me

That's it. Watch what changes.

---

### Run the DELTA Checklist
For any current AI project, ask:

- [ ] **Dignity**: Does this honor human worth?
- [ ] **Embodiment**: Does this respect physical/relational reality?
- [ ] **Love**: Does this foster genuine care?
- [ ] **Transcendence**: Does this point beyond utility?
- [ ] **Agency**: Does this preserve human responsibility?

If any answer is "no" — that's your focus area.
