## 1. Market Landscape Analysis

### Key Players & Offerings

**Elicit** ($12-79/month)
- Academic focus, systematic reviews
- 138+ million papers database
- Automated data extraction, PRISMA workflows
- Tiered pricing: Basic (free), Plus ($12), Pro ($49), Team ($79/user)

**Scispace** (~$20/month)
- PDF comprehension and writing assistance
- Literature review sections with paper summaries
- Free plan limits to 5 papers analyzed

**Consensus** ($8.99/month)
- Quick evidence-based answers with Yes/No responses
- Scientific agreement assessment
- Free tier available

**Paperguide AI** ($12/month)
- All-in-one AI research assistant
- Literature review with top 10 papers analysis
- Advanced filtering capabilities

## 3. Market Gaps & Opportunities

### Pricing Gaps
1. **Mid-market gap:** Between $12-49/month, few options exist
2. **Small business affordability:** Most tools target academics
3. **Pay-per-use model:** Missing from current offerings
4. **Volume discounts:** Limited options for high-volume users

### Feature Gaps
1. **Cross-domain synthesis:** Academic focus leaves business needs underserved
2. **Real-time data integration:** Limited with market data, news, social media
3. **Collaboration features:** Basic team features, lacking advanced workflows
4. **Customization limitations:** Free plans severely restrict options

### Market Segment Gaps
1. **Business professionals:** Most tools target academics
2. **Consultants:** Lack specialized consulting workflows
3. **Small teams:** High minimum seats for team plans
4. **Non-English markets:** Limited support available

## 4. Customer Willingness to Pay Analysis

### Sector-Based Budgets
- **Pharma/Healthcare:** Highest ($50,000-500,000+ for clinical research)
- **Tech/Startups:** Moderate ($10,000-100,000)
- **Academic/Non-profit:** Lower ($5,000-50,000)

### Decision Factors
1. **ROI focus:** Businesses pay more for revenue-driving research
2. **Risk mitigation:** Higher willingness for risk-reducing research
3. **Strategic value:** Premium pricing for major decision research
